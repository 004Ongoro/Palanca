Our Story

Welcome to Palanca Safari & Travel, where adventure meets passion. Founded in 2024 and proudly based in Angola, we are more than just a travel company; we are storytellers, experience-makers, and proud ambassadors of Africa's breathtaking beauty.

Born out of deep love for wildlife, culture, and the raw landscapes of Angola and beyond, Palanca Safari & Travel was created to offer travellerssomething trully special: tailor-made journeys that leave lasting memories.

Our Mission

To deliver authentic, unforgettable travel experiencesthat celebrate the beauty of Angola, Africa and the world; while promoting environmental conservation, cultural appreciation, and personalized service.
